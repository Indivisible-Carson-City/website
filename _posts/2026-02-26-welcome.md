We're thrilled to launch the new Indivisible Carson City website! This will be our hub for sharing news, upcoming events, and ways you can get involved in our community.

## What to Expect

Here on our blog, you'll find:

- **Event recaps** from our meetings and community actions
- **Action alerts** when it's time to call your representatives or show up
- **Volunteer spotlights** highlighting the amazing people who make our group run
- **Local news** and analysis that affects our community

## Stay Connected

Bookmark this site and check back regularly for updates. We'll be posting about upcoming events, sharing resources, and keeping you informed about what's happening in Carson City and beyond.

Together, we're stronger. Welcome aboard!
