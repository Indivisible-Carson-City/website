## Who We Are

Indivisible Carson City is a local grassroots group of neighbors and community members working together to promote progressive values, civic engagement, and accountability in Carson City, Nevada.

We are part of the national [Indivisible](https://indivisible.org) movement — a grassroots network of thousands of local groups that have come together to resist attacks on our democratic institutions and build a more just society.

## What We Do

- **Attend and organize** town halls, public meetings, and community forums
- **Register voters** and help neighbors make their voices heard at the ballot box
- **Advocate** for policies that support working families, protect civil rights, and strengthen our community
- **Connect** with local, state, and federal representatives to hold them accountable

## Get Involved

Whether you have five minutes or five hours a week, there's a place for you. Join us at an upcoming event or reach out to learn more about how you can make a difference right here in Carson City.
